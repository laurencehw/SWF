# United States Strategic Investment Fund
## Executive Brief

*A one-page summary of the white paper "A Strategic Investment Fund for America"*

---

### The Challenge

The United States faces a strategic investment gap. China deploys **$1+ trillion** through Belt and Road. Gulf states leverage **$2+ trillion** in sovereign wealth. The US has no comparable tool for strategic capital deployment at scale.

### Key Design Features

| Element | Design Choice | Rationale |
|---------|--------------|-----------|
| **Governance** | 9-member board, 4 independent (voting majority) | Insulate from politicization |
| **Terms** | 7 years, staggered | Span election cycles |
| **Legal Basis** | Congressional statute | Constitutional protection |
| **Compliance** | Santiago Principles | International credibility |
| **Oversight** | GAO + Inspector General | Democratic accountability |

---

### Funding Pathway

| Phase | Timeline | Source | Amount |
|-------|----------|--------|--------|
| **1** | Years 1-2 | Congressional appropriation | $50B initial + $10B/yr |
| **2** | Years 3-5 | + Dedicated revenues (minerals, spectrum) | $15-20B/yr |
| **3** | Years 6-10 | + Asset monetization | $20-30B/yr |
| **4** | Years 11+ | Investment returns + ongoing flows | Self-sustaining |

---

### Addressing Key Concerns

| Concern | Response |
|---------|----------|
| **Politicization** | Independent board majority; statutory prohibitions; fiduciary duties |
| **Deficit funding** | Phased approach; dedicated revenues; returns must exceed borrowing costs |
| **Market distortion** | Size limits (~1% of US equity market); gradual deployment |
| **Mission creep** | Dual-account separation; 10-year sunset/reauthorization |
| **Corruption** | Multiple oversight layers; transparency; whistleblower protections |

### The Bottom Line

A well-designed US sovereign wealth fund could:

- **Compete** with Chinese strategic capital globally
- **Secure** critical supply chains and minerals
- **Catalyze** domestic infrastructure investment
- **Generate** long-term returns for Americans
- **Strengthen** alliances through co-investment

**But design is destiny.** Without robust governance, the fund risks becoming a vehicle for political patronage. This proposal provides a blueprint for maximizing strategic value while minimizing politicization risks.
